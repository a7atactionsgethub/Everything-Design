Silhouette footers are more cinematic and atmospheric than the others.

The core idea is:

> using a strong silhouetted visual form as the emotional anchor of the footer.

Usually:

* skyline
* figure
* architecture
* mountain
* machinery
* vehicle
* city mass
* abstract structure
* environmental shape

But treated with restraint.

The silhouette should feel:

* iconic
* moody
* graphic
* integrated into composition
* atmospheric rather than illustrative

Strong references:

* [Awwwards Cinematic Sites](https://www.awwwards.com/websites/experimental/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
* [Footer Design](https://www.footer.design/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
* [Godly Website Inspiration](https://godly.website/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
* [Minimal Gallery](https://minimal.gallery/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

The best silhouette footers usually:

* blend image + typography
* use deep contrast
* place silhouettes near horizon lines
* create cinematic depth
* use subtle layering and fade systems
* avoid looking like illustrations pasted into a footer
